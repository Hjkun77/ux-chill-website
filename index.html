UX

&CHILL

NOV 05 | 14 | 20 | 25

Learn more about innovation, design-thinking, and all about user experience (UX) in this three-day workshop which will unfold over the month of November. UX&Chill aims to promote UX and its uses to various demographics, and invite experts to share about their expertise and journey in the UX industry. Make art work. Chill with UX.
